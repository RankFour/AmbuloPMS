What types of businesses are suitable for your commercial spaces?

Our commercial units are perfect for various businesses including retail stores, restaurants, beauty salons, medical clinics, professional offices, and service-based businesses. We welcome entrepreneurs from all industries who are looking for strategic locations in Silang, Cavite.

What is the typical lease term and rental rates?

We offer flexible lease terms starting from 1-year contracts. Rental rates vary depending on unit size, location within the property, and specific business requirements. Contact us for detailed pricing information and to discuss customized lease agreements.

Are utilities and maintenance included in the rent?

Basic maintenance of common areas is included in the rent. Utilities such as electricity and water are typically separate and metered individually for each unit. We provide detailed breakdowns during the leasing consultation process.
