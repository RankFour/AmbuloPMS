1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to users, tenants, administrators, and visitors interacting with the Ambulo Property Management System (“Ambulo PMS”).

2. Information We Collect (During Onboarding)

2.1 Account & Profile Data

Name, email address, phone numbers, profile photo, emergency contact, and billing address details required to create and manage your account.

2.2 Lease & Transactional Data

Lease terms, unit identifiers, payment history, invoices, documents uploaded (e.g., IDs, agreements).

2.3 System Usage & Technical Data

Log events, access timestamps, device/browser type, IP address (for security and fraud monitoring).

2.4 Support & Messaging Data

Messages or notes exchanged during setup (e.g., verification queries) and related metadata.

2.5 Optional Marketing & Feedback

Survey responses, feature usage insights, and voluntary feedback forms to improve the platform.

If sensitive personal data is provided for verification (e.g., government ID), it is used solely for that purpose and handled with enhanced safeguards.

3. How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and secure tenant, property, and payment management functionality.
  • Generate invoices, track payment status, and enable communication between users and staff.
  • Provide support and respond to inquiries or maintenance requests.
  • Improve performance, usability, and reliability of the platform.
  • Comply with accounting, tax, and regulatory obligations.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Processing relies on: performance of a contract (lease/service agreement), legitimate interests (platform security, onboarding efficiency), consent (optional communications), and legal obligations (financial record keeping).

5. Sharing & Disclosure

We may share limited data:

  • With service providers (cloud hosting, payment processors) under strict confidentiality.
  • With property owners and authorized staff for operational purposes.
  • For legal compliance, subpoenas, or to protect rights and safety.

We do not sell personal information.

6. Data Retention

Account, lease, and financial records are retained for required statutory periods (often 5–7 years) or as needed to support active tenant relationships. Backups follow scheduled lifecycle management.

7. Security

We implement layered safeguards: encrypted transport (HTTPS), role-based access, audit logging, and routine patching. You should also maintain a strong, unique password to protect your account.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on applicable law, you may request: access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection to certain processing. You may also withdraw consent where applicable without affecting prior lawful processing.

9. Cookies & Tracking

We use strictly necessary cookies for session management and security. Optional analytics cookies (if enabled) help improve features. You can manage preferences via browser settings.

10. International Transfers

Data may be processed or stored in jurisdictions with different data protection laws. We apply safeguards (e.g., contractual protections) when transferring data internationally.

11. Children’s Privacy

The platform is not directed to children under 18. If we learn a minor provided personal data without authorization, we will take steps to delete it.

12. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via dashboard notice or email. Continued use after updates signifies acceptance.

13. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries, requests, or complaints: support@ambulo.com
